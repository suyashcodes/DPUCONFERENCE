Paper Registration

  • Registration fee includes a conference kit, e-Conference Proceedings-in PEN drive, lunch and high tea for both the days.
  • Last date of registration for attendee is the same as for the author and no conference kit will be provided to the accompanying person.
  • Certificate of Conference Participation/Presentation will be provided to only the first authors of paper in Hard Copy form.
  • If a paper is authored by more than one author, it is mandatory for at least one author to register for the conference.

Paper Guidelines

  • Presentation will be for a maximum of 8-10 minutes followed by a Q-A session.
  • All presentations must be done using MS PowerPoint Presentation.
  • All the participants have to join 15 minutes prior to the scheduled time slot.
  • Further information will be updated time to time on conference website.
